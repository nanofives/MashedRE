## Interpretation

| Return | Meaning in context |
|--------|--------------------|
| 0 | Point is in front of plane (positive side) — triangle skipped |
| 1 | Point is exactly on plane — triangle skipped |
| 2 | Point is behind plane (negative side) — triangle included |
