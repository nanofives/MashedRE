## Purpose

Per-vehicle wheel drivetrain and CVT (continuously-variable-transmission) update. Computes speed magnitude, processes 5 gear/CVT candidates, manages gear-shift timer, applies throttle and braking forces from wheel block entries, accumulates torque vectors, and applies final angular velocity damping including lateral friction. Resolves [UNCERTAIN U-3564] and [UNCERTAIN U-3565] from vehicle_update_d2.
