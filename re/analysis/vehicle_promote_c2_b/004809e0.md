## Purpose

Vehicle respawn teleport. Called when (&DAT_008815b4)[param_1*0x341] == 1 (vehicle is marked "placed" but needs teleport-back). Caches current physics velocity (scaled), reads spawn point transform from FUN_0046d4a0, applies it to physics body, subtracts velocity bias, re-adds body to world. Clears the placed flag and resets invincibility timer.
