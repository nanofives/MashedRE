## Purpose

Aerodynamic pitch stabilizer for airborne vehicles. When vehicle_struct+0x9E0 == 0.0 (airborne) and +0x9F0 == 0 (no grounded contact), computes pitch/roll correction torques from the difference between vehicle's up-vector and world up, scaled by a delta-time and velocity factor. When +0x9F0 != 0 (some grounded state), zeroes angular velocity and normalizes linear velocity. Resolves [UNCERTAIN U-3565] from vehicle_update_d2.
