## Purpose

Vehicle respawn placement. Clears physics body velocity, moves vehicle to spawn point from FUN_0046d4a0, applies velocity bias, flags vehicle as placed, sets respawn invincibility timer. Complementary to VehicleRespawnTeleport (0x004809e0); this function is called when (&DAT_008815b4)[param_1*0x341] == 0 (vehicle not yet marked placed) vs. the teleport function which is called when it's 1.
