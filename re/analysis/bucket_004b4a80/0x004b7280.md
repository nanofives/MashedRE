- Else manages the **reference table** (L[0x14] array, L[0x15] count, L[0x16] free-list-head, L[0x18] mem-counter):

- Returns the slot index.
- Pattern: **lua_ref(L, lock)** / `luaL_ref` from Lua 5.0 (creates a reference into the registry/refs table).
