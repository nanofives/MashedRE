## Purpose

Per-course full teardown (CourseAssetDestroy). Walks the course struct at `param_1` using
counts from descriptor `param_2`, destroying every loaded resource: special-object atomics,
collision BSPs (+0x10090/+0x100a0), particle slots (+0x100b0 ×16), MTS/sky-shadow (+0x100f0 ×8),
light/sky atomics (+0x105d8..+0x105e4), sky-shadow clump (+0x105dc), the 64-slot model array
(+0x10110), camera path (+0x105f0), general anims (+0x105b0 ×8), world BSP (+0x105d4),
particle-system clumps (+0x10040), splines (+0x10080), UVA dicts (+0x10618 ×8), and the
course texture dictionary (+0x105d0). Logs "World Destroyed" and "TextureDictionary Destroyed".

## Shape

`param_2` = course descriptor (supplies loop counts at +4/+0x1c/+0x20/+0x6c).
