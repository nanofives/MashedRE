## Purpose

Builds one collision-sector record from an `RpWorldSector` (`param_2`). Reads the sector's
triangle count (`+0x10` of its geometry at `+0x18`), vertex base, material list, etc.; allocates
a normals scratch (`tri*0xc`), computes face normals (`FUN_0047be80`) and half-edge adjacency
(`FUN_0047bcc0`), writes triangle data into `param_1[0..]`, edge/portal data into
`param_1+0xf2`-region, bounds via `FUN_004c3d90` into `param_1+0x24`, and finalizes via
`FUN_004b53b0(param_1+0x4e, param_1+0x24, param_1[0x53])`. Output record stride matches the
0x56-dword sector record consumed by FUN_0047c0f0.
