- Software-mode (`caps[+0x50] & 8`): toggles bit 3 (0x08) on `audioSource[+0x11c][+0xcc]` (software-voice flags).
- Hardware-mode: toggles bit 0x800 on `audioSource[+0x28]` (the same bit checked by FUN_005b8c40's Play-with-loop path).
- `loopFlag != 0` → set; `== 0` → clear.
