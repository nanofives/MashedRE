## Mechanical description
- Two-arg position setter (int audioSource, int sampleFrame).
- Software-mode (`*(audioSource[+0x94])[+0x50] & 8`): writes 0 to voice[+0x88] and sampleFrame to voice[+0x8c] at `audioSource[+0x11c]`.
- Hardware-mode: computes `byteOffset = (caps[+0x38] >> 3) * caps[+0x39] * sampleFrame` (sample-frame × blockAlign), stores at audioSource[+0x8c], +0x90 and the raw frame count at +0x88. ORs `audioSource[+0x28] |= 0x400` (mark "position rewind needed").
