## Mechanical description
- Writes the low 6 bits (`& 0x03ff` mask applied via complement) of an octant-table entry at `this + 0x9820 + ((octant + node*10) * 2)`.
- Computes `iVar1 = (param_3 & 0xffff) + (param_2 & 0xffff) * 10` — the same 10-entry-per-node octant child table index used by FUN_00564c80, FUN_005646c0, FUN_00564310.
- Read-modify-write: `slot = (slot & 0xfc00) | (param_4 & 0x3ff)`.
- Helper for octree split fan-out (FUN_00564c80 calls this when redistributing primitives across 8 new octants).

## Uncertainties
- [UNCERTAIN] The `0xfc00`/`0x3ff` split — high bits encode the 5-bit primitive count (0x7c00) plus the leaf flag (0x8000), and the low 10 bits hold either the leaf-chain head OR the child node id; this writer overwrites the low 10 bits unconditionally.
