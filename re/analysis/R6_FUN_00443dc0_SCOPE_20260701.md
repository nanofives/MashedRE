3. Refine: eval spline at t=0.01(0x3c23d70a)/0.99(0x3f7d70a4)/1.0/0.0 at iVar10 and

   between the two brackets to find the closest-approach t.
4. Forward walk 16 samples: advance param by `_DAT_005cc9a0`, wrapping idx at count;
   eval spline; store point → `local_140[2i]`; compute normalized direction dot

5. Pick max-dot sample index → write `outXZ[0..1] = local_140[best*2..*2+1]`.
6. Wall-check retry loop: ray-march vehXZ→outXZ (`FUN_004c3bf0` length, step
