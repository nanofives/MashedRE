## 6. Strategic conclusion (adopted 2026-06-09, user-ratified)

Supply-driven coverage (sweep → promote by percentage) has hit measured exhaustion;
the standalone exe is both the stated end-goal and the locus of recent wins. Decision:
**standalone-first, demand-driven RE** — port what the next vertical slice needs, verify
every port with diff-original (C3/C4 as byproduct), measure progress in demonstrable
capabilities. C4 debt resolved by harness re-validation lane. Full repair pass as Phase R0.
End state: **fully playable port** (P-DoD as written). See `ROADMAP.md` v2.
