## Mechanical description

- Signature `void f(RwImage* dst, RwImage* src)`.
- Combines the same-depth fast-path (0x004ceaf0) with the cross-depth
  converter (0x004ceba0) into one function operating on already-
  allocated `dst`:
  - **Same-depth branch** (`dst->depth == src->depth`):
    - Copy palette (if both have one and depth < 9), same shape
      as 0x004ceaf0.
    - Copy pixels row-by-row with `((depth+7)>>3) * width` bytes.
  - **Cross-depth branch**: same packed-depth switch as 0x004ceba0
    (0x808/0x404/0x408 no-op or byte-copy; 0x420/0x820 palette-expand
    to 32bpp; 0x2020 no-op; else RwError 0x80000009).
- **Post-copy flag stitch**: `dst->flags = (dst->flags & ~2) | (src->flags & 2)`
  — propagates bit 1 ("owns-palette" or similar) from src to dst,
  clears bit 1 in the in-between mid-assignment.
