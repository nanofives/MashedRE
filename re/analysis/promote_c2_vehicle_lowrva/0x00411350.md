`FUN_004a2c48` is the "float→int with FPU ST0 side-effect" rounder seen elsewhere.
The caller passes `raw_ticks` which is implicitly loaded onto ST0 before the
call (decompiler `extraout_ST0`). Then iterates secs/60 → mins, leaving secs
in 0..59.
