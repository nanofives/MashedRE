If the replay-obj pointer is non-null, fetches the checkpoint timestamp at
`replay_obj + 0x17c + idx*4` and runs it through TimeFormat (FUN_00411350).
Otherwise zeroes the mins+frac outs (secs is implicitly zero from `param_4`
NOT being touched — note: actually only writes 2 of 3 zero outputs; secs is
not cleared in the null path. Decompiler confirms only `*param_3` and `*param_4`
get explicit zero stores in the early-out).
