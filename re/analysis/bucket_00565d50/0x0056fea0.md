## Mechanical description

- Signature: `void FUN_0056fea0(float *param_1, float *param_2, float *param_3, float *param_4)`.
- Reads two quaternions `param_1[0..3]` and `param_2[0..3]`.
- Computes 16 cross-products `fVar1..fVar16` of all xy/xz/...xw pairs.
- Output 1: `param_3[0..3]` = `q1 * q2` (standard Hamilton product) of the quaternion product.
- Output 2: `param_4[0..11]` = 4×3 matrix from `q1 * (q2 cross [i,j,k]) / 2` — partial derivatives of `q1 ⊗ q2 ⊗ v` w.r.t. `v`'s 3 components.
  - This is the **G matrix** used in quaternion-rate constraints (qdot = 0.5 * G * omega).
- Net effect: **build constraint-Jacobian G-matrix** for quaternion-velocity constraints (joint orientation, motor angles).
