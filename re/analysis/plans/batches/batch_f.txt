   physics layer should be a caller of those, not a callee.
5. If you find ≥1 candidate top-level physics function → PROCEED to sweep.

   "after vehicle_dynamics has its tick chain mapped."

   subset. Cap 20. Overflow → cont1.
9. Per-RVA C1 plates. Subsystem=physics on every row.
10. Identify the physics-state struct (separate from vehicle state — the
    integrator's working buffers: per-step Δv, accumulator, contact list).
    Draft re/analysis/structs/physics_state.md if a candidate emerges.
11. Shutdown + scribe-queue append.

# Output
- EITHER: closure with DEFERRED row + REPORT.md explaining search
