## Interpretation
Music channel group volume setter. Sets `param_1+0x38` (volume field) to `param_2`, then marks all nodes in a circular linked list as "dirty" (bit 6 at node+0x14). Also propagates the volume to a secondary object's field +0x30 if one is present at `param_1+0x11c`.
