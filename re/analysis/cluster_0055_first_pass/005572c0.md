triangle-strip and uploads to D3D via the RW immediate-mode draw
primitives (FUN_004cd070..140). The tail-recursion through
param_1[5] processes the linked-list of sub-paths.

geometric tween (FUN_00557110) and color tween (FUN_00556e90) data
into per-vertex color/UV. This is the rendering core that the layout
