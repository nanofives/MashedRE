  (`qh_setdelnth`), toggle the `0x1000` (toporient) flag alternately (`local_c`),
  set the `0x400` (newfacet) flag, append to a temp set.
- Then links every facet to all others as neighbors (`qh_setbase`/`FUN_00583e40`)
