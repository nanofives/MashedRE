## Mechanical description

**Qhull 2002.1 `qh_triangulate_facet`** — confirmed by string
`s_qh_triangulate_facet__triangulat_...` and a direct call to the already-named
qhull symbol `qh_printfacet`.

Triangulates one non-simplicial facet `param_1` into a fan of simplicial facets:
clears neighbor flags, optionally recomputes the centrum (`qh_getcentrum`) when the
facet plane is near-degenerate, builds the fan from the facet's first vertex via
`qh_makenew_nonsimplicial` (FUN_0059bda0), then for each new facet copies the
parent's normal/centrum (or recomputes via `qh_copypoints`/`qh_normalize` when
`DAT_00913ff0`/Qt set), inherits good/orientation flags (`0x200`, `0x20000`,
`0x80000`, `0x10000000`), and links it. Updates fan-count statistics
(`DAT_009135c4/cc/c8`), records the new-vertex list head into `*param_2`, and calls
`qh_resetlists`.
