## Signature

```c
void FUN_004dee20(undefined1 *param_1, undefined4 param_2);
```

- `param_1` — destination: 4-byte BGRA output.
- `param_2` — source: packed pixel; byte 1 (bits 15..8) is the luminance value.

## Mechanical description

Extracts `luma = (byte)(param_2 >> 8)`. Writes `luma` to `param_1[0]`, `param_1[1]`, `param_1[2]`; writes 0xFF to `param_1[3]` (full alpha). Implements greyscale-to-BGRA8 expansion for D3DFORMAT 0x51 (L8 luminance).
