## Purpose

Bounds-checked destruction-state getter. Reads the per-car destruction state integer from
`DAT_008815b0 + param_1 * 0xD04` (+0x0C within the per-car block). Companion to FUN_0046c790 (writer)
and FUN_0046c7b0 (alive-flag getter at +0x00).

## Shape

**Signature:** `undefined4 FUN_0046c770(uint param_1)` — param_1 = car index (0–15)
**Returns:** dword at vehicle[param_1]+0x0C, or `0xffffffff` if param_1 > 15

**Reads:**
- `DAT_008815b0` — per-car destruction-state field (byte offset +0x0C from block base `DAT_008815a4`)
- Stride 0x341 DWORDs (= 0xD04 bytes) per vehicle slot
