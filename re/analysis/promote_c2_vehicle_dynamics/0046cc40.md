## Purpose

WheelTerrainContactClassifier. Iterates the RW broadphase terrain contact batch
(`DAT_0088e60c` entry count, each entry 36 floats) and for each terrain triangle performs
a depth + approach-speed test against 4 wheel positions (`DAT_0088e624`). Accepts contacts
whose depth falls within `[_DAT_005cea5c .. _DAT_005cc564]` and whose approach speed
exceeds `_DAT_005cc99c`. Writes face normal, depth, and surface metadata into the
per-wheel contact slot at `vehicle + 0x65 + slot*0x31`. Stops once `DAT_0088e650` reaches
4 (all 4 wheels classified).
