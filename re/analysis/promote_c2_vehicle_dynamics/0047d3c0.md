## Purpose

Creates the RWP37 physics world and registers 4 vehicle rigid bodies. Sets world gravity
(Y=−0.1f), computes CoM offsets from vehicle position delta globals and broadcasts to
4 per-vehicle slots, sets friction (0.45f), restitution (0.5f), damping (0.01f), mass
(1.5f). Loop 4× per vehicle: creates physics body + collision body, attaches visual,
assigns to world slot, places at (0.5f, −10.0f, i*DAT_005cd074), registers in physics
scene, sets shape flags.
