## Purpose

Reads EAX[0x14] (dword-index) to look up skin/colour handles A and B from `DAT_005f32b0`
and `DAT_005f32b4` (stride 8 bytes per entry), writes them to EAX[+0x00] and EAX[+0x04].
Then zeroes fields at byte offsets +0x34, +0x38, +0x3c, +0x40, +0x44, +0x4c; sets
+0x64 = 0xffffffff (no-target sentinel).
