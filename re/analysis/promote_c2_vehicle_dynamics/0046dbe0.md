## Purpose

Returns the race-position integer (0=1st, 1=2nd, 2=3rd, 3=4th) for vehicle slot `param_1`.
Reads `DAT_008815a8 + param_1 * 0x341` (offset +0x00 of the vehicle stride). Used by
VehicleRubberBandSpeedModifier (0x00442ce0) to compare race rankings.
