## Purpose

Triangle face normal calculator. Given three vertices (EAX=apex, param_1=B, param_2=C),
computes a normalised cross product `(apex−B) × (apex−C)` and writes the unit normal to
`*ESI`. Silent no-op if the triangle is degenerate (dot product below threshold).
Called by VehicleWheelContactSolver (0x0046f6c0) to re-derive the face normal from stored
triangle vertices during the orientation correction pass.

## Shape

```c
void FUN_0046c5f0(float *param_1, float *param_2);
// EAX  = apex vertex   float[3]
// ESI  = output normal float[3]  (written on success only)
// param_1 = second vertex float[3]
// param_2 = third vertex float[3]
```

**Reads:** EAX[0..2], param_1[0..2], param_2[0..2]
**Writes:** ESI[0..2] (unit normal)
