## Purpose

Computes per-wheel contact RW query, drive torque from throttle input, steer torque per wheel from velocity-delta dot product, and spring force vectors. Also updates the position history ping-pong buffer and velocity damping/airborne flag.

## Shape

**Signature:** `void VehicleWheelForceIntegrator(float param_1, undefined4 param_2)`
- param_1 = delta time (ticks/ms)
- param_2 = transform matrix passed through to FUN_004c3df0
- EDI = implicit vehicle struct pointer (`unaff_EDI`)
**Returns:** void

**Reads (from EDI-relative offsets):**
- `EDI+0x278` — contact mode float (0x40800000 = 4.0f = all 4 wheels grounded)
- `EDI+0x279` — throttle float
- `EDI+0x2B4` — ping-pong ring index (0 or 1)
- `EDI+0x2B5..0x2BA` — position history (2 × XYZ)
- `EDI+0x60..0x62` — right-dir XYZ (wheel 0)
- `EDI+0x2C0..2C2` — random impulse per wheel (set by FUN_00472650)

**Writes (to EDI-relative offsets):**
- `EDI+0x83, 0xB4, 0xE5, 0x116` — steer values for wheels 0..3
- `EDI+0x2C8` — airborne flag
- `EDI+0x2B4/5/6/7` — updated ping-pong index and position
