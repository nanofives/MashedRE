## Purpose

16-vehicle dispatcher. Initializes physics-scale globals, handles game-mode-1/2 special cases (player-reset/tournament-reset), sorts vehicles by a per-car float, then runs a do-while substep loop that applies per-vehicle control, dynamics integration, and collision resolution. Returns 1 when substep count reaches 0.
