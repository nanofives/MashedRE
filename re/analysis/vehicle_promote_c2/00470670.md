## Purpose

Per-vehicle control input integrator. Clears control output slots, computes speed-normalized forward projection, reads boost timers, then applies throttle/brake forces through a full scale pipeline, writes to force accumulator history ring, and delegates to wheel force, drivetrain, and aero sub-steps.
