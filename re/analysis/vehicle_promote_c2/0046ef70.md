## Purpose

Wheel contact spring/damper force resolver. Iterates 24 contact records (stride 0x40 bytes) from EDI+0x4B4, computes spring and friction forces per contact, accumulates angular impulse for secondary contacts, and writes the scaled results to linear and angular velocity fields.
